UEFA EURO 2024 in Germany


(Jump to TIMETABLES)

Three-time winners Germany will stage the UEFA European Championship in 2024, marking the 17th edition of the tournament. This decision was made at a UEFA Executive Committee meeting in Nyon on 27 September 2018, with Türkiye being the only other bid. While West Germany hosted the 1988 edition, this will be the first time unified Germany will stage the UEFA European Championship since reunification. The 2006 FIFA World Cup was also hosted in Germany.

When is EURO 2024?

The UEFA EURO 2024 final tournament is scheduled from 14 June to 14 July 2024. The group stage will run until 26 June, followed by the knockout stage starting on 29 June.

As the host nation, Germany are seeded in Group A and will occupy position A1, playing the opening match against Scotland at the Munich Football Arena on Friday 14 June.

Who has qualified for EURO 2024?

The draw for the final tournament was held in Hamburg on Saturday 2 December.

  • Group A: Germany, Scotland, Hungary, Switzerland
  • Group B: Spain, Croatia, Italy, Albania
  • Group C: Slovenia, Denmark, Serbia, England
  • Group D: Poland, Netherlands, Austria, France
  • Group E: Belgium, Slovakia, Romania, Ukraine
  • Group F: Türkiye, Georgia, Portugal, Czechia

EURO 2024 Host Cities and Venues

The ten venues chosen to host the games at the tournament include nine stadiums used during the 2006 World Cup plus the Düsseldorf Arena. Notably, the Munich Football Arena will be staging matches for the second consecutive EURO, having been one of the 11 arenas used during UEFA EURO 2020.

  • Berlin: Olympiastadion Berlin (71,000 capacity)
  • Cologne: Cologne Stadium (43,000)
  • Dortmund: BVB Stadion Dortmund (62,000)
  • Düsseldorf: Düsseldorf Arena (47,000)
  • Frankfurt: Frankfurt Arena (47,000)
  • Gelsenkirchen: Arena AufSchalke (50,000)
  • Hamburg: Volksparkstadion Hamburg (49,000)
  • Leipzig: Leipzig Stadium (40,000)
  • Munich: Munich Football Arena (66,000)
  • Stuttgart: Stuttgart Arena (51,000)

Key Dates for EURO 2024

Final tournament dates include the opening game on 14 June at the Munich Football Arena and the final on 14 July at the Olympiastadion Berlin.

Final of UEFA EURO 2024

The final will be held at Berlin's Olympiastadion on Sunday, 14 July 2024.
